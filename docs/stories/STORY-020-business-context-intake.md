## User Story

As a business owner, I want business goals, business PRDs, workflows, roles,
risks, and acceptance criteria to be captured as structured inputs so that the
Agent Harness is built around real business intent.

## Implementation Plan

1. Define a business context intake template.
2. Include goals, users, workflows, roles, constraints, risks, and success
   metrics.
3. Map each accepted business requirement to harness capability areas.
4. Mark assumptions that require business owner confirmation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Intake captures business goals and success criteria.
- Intake captures business workflows and roles.
- Intake captures acceptance criteria and risks.
- Intake output can be referenced by the harness blueprint.
